Costa del Sol Area Guide

Estepona, a more relaxed coastal rhythm

A walkable town, long seafront and strong new-build supply, with a calmer pace than central Marbella.

Living in Estepona

Coastal living with room to breathe

The main choice is between the walkable town centre, beachside communities and the New Golden Mile. The latter offers newer residential projects between Estepona and Marbella, but daily life is generally more car-dependent.

01

Best For

Buyers seeking a practical coastal base with more value range than central Marbella.

02

Town or New Golden Mile

The centre offers walkability; newer coastal communities offer pools, space and managed grounds.

03

Daily Life

The old town, promenade, schools, healthcare and shops remain active throughout the year.

04

Access

The A-7 connects Estepona with Marbella, Gibraltar and Malaga Airport; journey times vary with traffic.
