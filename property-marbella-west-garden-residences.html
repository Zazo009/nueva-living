Overview

Resort-scale amenities in a walkable coastal setting

Marbella West Garden Residences brings together 139 homes around planted courtyards, pools and spaces for wellness, work and family life. It is a large, amenity-led project, but the architecture is arranged to keep outdoor living and natural light at the centre of each home.

The location is practical as well as attractive: the beach is described by the developer as a 10-minute walk, Puerto Banus is around five minutes by car, and San Pedro's everyday services are close by.

Why This Project

Why this Marbella West project stands apart

This is a strong fit for buyers who want the services of a resort without giving up the independence of a private home or the convenience of being close to San Pedro and Puerto Banus.

Who it is for

Buyers looking for a primary home, an easy second residence or a family base with wellness, social and work facilities on site.

What makes it different

The amenity programme goes beyond a standard pool and gym, adding a full wellness hub, rooftop lounge, business space, cinema, kids' room and pet facilities.

Why location matters

The setting connects a walkable beach lifestyle with quick access to Puerto Banus, San Pedro, Guadalmina and several leading golf courses.

Why check availability

The mix ranges from efficient 1-bedroom homes to duplex Sky Villas, so floor, orientation, terrace and release status make a meaningful difference.

Architecture

Light-first architecture wrapped in Mediterranean planting

Huete Arquitectos has organised the buildings around landscaped spaces, with rounded terraces, integrated planters and a warm palette of stone, sand and wood tones. The layouts open living areas towards generous terraces and keep movement between indoors and outdoors simple.

The specification includes high-performance glazing, motorised bedroom shutters, ducted climate control, aerothermal hot water and an A-A energy rating. Selected corner homes and all Sky Villas add private pools.

  • Architecture by Huete Arquitectos and landscaping by Laura Pou Studio.
  • Large-format porcelain surfaces inside and on the terraces.
  • Thermal-break aluminium joinery and high-performance double glazing.
  • Private pools in Iconic Residences and Sky Villas.

Lifestyle

A home base designed for more than weekends

The amenity mix is designed to make longer stays and everyday life easier: train, work, recover, meet friends or walk to the coast without building the day around a car.

Wellness at home

The wellness hub includes a gym, indoor lap pool, sauna, steam bath, hydromassage, contrast pool and red-light therapy.

Space for real routines

From rooftop to beach

Investment Context

The practical case for Marbella West

The strongest case here is the combination of location, broad residence mix and unusually complete amenities. Any rental or resale plan still depends on the exact home, purchase cost, community fees and applicable rules.

Growing district

Marbella West is receiving new residential stock and infrastructure close to an established coastal lifestyle.

Broad buyer appeal

Long-stay usability

Selection matters

Before You View

What to confirm before you reserve

The developer material gives a clear initial framework. We still confirm the current commercial terms and recommend independent legal review before any reservation payment.

Project team

Developer entity: Residencial Puertalcantara S.L.; architecture by Huete Arquitectos; construction by Bonifacio Solis S.L.

Programme and licence

The building licence is stated as granted. Construction is listed as started in Q1 2026, with estimated completion in Q2 2029.

Payment structure

The supplied terms state a EUR 6,600 reservation, 30% plus VAT on the private contract and 70% plus VAT on completion.

Buyer protection

The material states that amounts paid are to be protected by a bank guarantee. Your lawyer should verify the guarantee and contract before funds are released.
