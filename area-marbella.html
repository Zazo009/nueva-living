Costa del Sol Area Guide

Marbella, the coast's best-known address

Beach life, established neighbourhoods, international schools and year-round energy come together in one of southern Europe's most recognised residential markets.

Living in Marbella

A place that works beyond the summer

Marbella combines a relaxed Mediterranean setting with the services buyers need for longer stays or full-time living. The beach, restaurants, private healthcare and international schools are all part of everyday life here.

The area is varied. Marbella East feels greener and more residential, the Golden Mile is closer to the centre and Puerto Banus, while hillside neighbourhoods offer more privacy and open views.

01

Coast & Beach Clubs

A long coastline with family beaches, beach clubs and year-round seafront dining.

02

Dining & Daily Life

A broad restaurant scene, international services, private healthcare and established shopping.

03

Schools & Families

Several international schools and residential neighbourhoods suited to full-time family life.

04

Golf & Leisure

Easy access to courses across Marbella East, Nueva Andalucia and Benahavis.

05

International Access

Around 40 to 45 minutes from Malaga Airport, depending on the neighbourhood.

06

Property Choice

Apartments, penthouses and villas across beachside, town and elevated settings.
