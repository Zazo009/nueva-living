Nueva Andalucía, life around Golf Valley

An established residential area close to Puerto Banus, built around golf, services and easy second-home living.

Living in Nueva Andalucía

Marbella convenience with more residential calm

Nueva Andalucia ranges from walkable pockets near local services to quieter, elevated parts of Golf Valley. Proximity to Puerto Banus is a major advantage, while traffic, views and walkability vary noticeably between streets and communities.
