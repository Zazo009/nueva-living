Our Approach

A Considered Approach
to New Developments

Nueva Living focuses solely on new-build and off-plan opportunities, giving buyers a clear, curated and informed route into the Costa del Sol property market. By specialising exclusively in new development, we offer a depth of knowledge no generalist agency can match.

01

Curated Developer Projects

We select only credible, well-positioned developments from established developers — eliminating noise and presenting only what truly merits consideration.

02

Buyer-Focused Advisory

Our role is to represent your interests. From first enquiry to completion, every recommendation is made with your goals and long-term value in mind.

03

Costa del Sol Market Insight

Deep local knowledge across pricing trends, micro-locations, developer track records and the evolving dynamics of the Andalusian new-build market.

Why New Development

The Advantages of
Buying New

Modern Design & Energy Efficiency

Contemporary architecture with the latest energy ratings, smart home systems and premium materials built to exacting European standards.

Flexible Payment Structures

Stage payments spread across construction phases reduce upfront capital requirements and allow buyers greater financial flexibility.

Developer Warranties

Structural guarantees, building warranties and new appliance cover provide the reassurance unavailable in the resale market.

Rental & Resale Potential

New builds consistently command premium rental yields and resale premiums throughout the Costa del Sol luxury market.

Early-Phase Pricing

Early reservation can secure the best units at launch pricing, with meaningful capital appreciation before completion.

Locations We Cover

The Costa del Sol
Area by Area

Marbella

The crown jewel of the Costa del Sol. From the Golden Mile to Sierra Blanca, Marbella commands the highest demand and strongest long-term value.

Estepona

A rapidly evolving market with significant infrastructure investment, strong developer pipeline and excellent price appreciation potential.

Benahavís

An elevated enclave above Marbella with panoramic views, gated communities and some of the most exclusive villa developments on the coast.

Nueva Andalucía

The Golf Valley of the Costa del Sol, surrounded by five championship courses and blending lifestyle with strong international buyer demand.

Mijas & Fuengirola

A diverse coastal corridor with new developments catering to buyers seeking value, lifestyle and excellent connectivity to Málaga.
