Costa del Sol Area Guide

Mijas & Fuengirola, connected coastal living

Two distinct coastal markets offering beaches, established services and easier access towards Malaga.

Living in Mijas & Fuengirola

More choice, easy access and daily convenience

Fuengirola is urban, walkable and rail-connected. Mijas ranges from the village and La Cala to golf and hillside communities, where a car is usually needed. The right choice depends more on the exact setting than the municipality name.

01

Best For

Buyers prioritising everyday convenience, transport links and a broader price range.

02

Fuengirola

Best suited to buyers wanting a walkable town, long seafront and rail access to Malaga Airport.

03

Mijas

La Cala, Mijas Pueblo and the inland golf communities offer very different lifestyles and access.

04

Property Choice

Urban apartments, coastal townhouses and hillside villas cover a wider range of needs.
