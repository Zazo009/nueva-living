Costa del Sol Area Guide

Mijas & Fuengirola, connected coastal living

Established services, beaches and direct links towards Malaga give this part of the coast a practical, lively character with a broad range of homes.

Living in Mijas & Fuengirola

More choice, easy access and daily convenience

Fuengirola offers a long seafront, an active town centre and direct rail connections to Malaga Airport. Mijas adds several distinct settings, from Mijas Pueblo to La Cala and newer hillside communities.

Together they offer a wider range of prices and property types than central Marbella, with strong everyday services and an established year-round population.

01

Transport

Fuengirola's rail connection gives direct access to Malaga Airport and the city.

02

Beaches

A long urban seafront in Fuengirola and a more relaxed coastal feel around La Cala de Mijas.

03

Everyday Life

Hospitals, schools, shopping and sports facilities support year-round living.

04

Mijas Pueblo

A traditional white village with mountain views and a different pace from the coast.

05

Golf

Several courses sit between Mijas Costa, La Cala and the inland hills.

06

Broader Choice

Apartments, townhouses and villas across urban, beachside and elevated locations.
