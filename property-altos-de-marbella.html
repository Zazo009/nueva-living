Overview

Elevated residences shaped by sea light

Altos de Marbella is for buyers who want more space, privacy and open views close to the Mediterranean.

It sits above the busier beachfront, with large terraces and wide windows that make the most of the light and sea views.

Why this project matters

Architecture

Architecture that lets the coast breathe

The architecture uses clean horizontal lines, shaded outdoor areas and wide windows that connect the rooms to the terraces.

The design is calm rather than showy, with a focus on proportion, shade, views and natural materials.

  • Large-format openings designed around light and sea orientation.
  • Generous terraces with a natural connection between indoor and outdoor spaces.
  • Warm natural textures balanced by crisp architectural volumes.
  • Private pool and garden options across selected residences.

Lifestyle

A quieter way to live near Marbella

Altos de Marbella keeps the coast close without putting you in the middle of the busiest areas. Marbella town, beach clubs, golf and everyday services are all a short drive away.

Sea-facing terraces

The terraces are made for morning light, evening meals and long coastal views.

More privacy

Easy to live in
