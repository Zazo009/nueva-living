Beachside ownership with daily convenience

Golden Coast Residences is positioned for buyers who want beach proximity, practical ownership and a residence profile that works for both personal use and selective rental demand.

The New Golden Mile setting gives the project a softer coastal rhythm than central Marbella while keeping services, beach clubs and Estepona access close.
