Overview

Layered hillside living with quiet elevation

Solenne is framed for buyers who want the privacy and elevation of Benahavís without losing access to Marbella, golf and coastal services.

The project language is calm and terrace-led: arrival, views and outdoor living are treated as part of the residential sequence rather than decoration.

Architecture

Architecture arranged around terrace rhythm

The architectural direction is hillside-sensitive: horizontal layers, landscaped edges and outdoor rooms designed to soften the transition from built form to landscape.

The result is a residence concept that prioritises shade, privacy, arrival and view composition rather than overstatement.

  • Layered terrace planning with generous exterior living space.
  • Soft Mediterranean landscaping integrated into the arrival sequence.
  • Panoramic glazing used for light and orientation.
  • Boutique scale with a private residential feel.
