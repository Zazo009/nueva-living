Overview

Hillside homes with space and open views

Solenne is for buyers who want the privacy and views of Benahavís while staying close to Marbella, golf and the coast.

The homes are planned around generous terraces, a calm arrival and outdoor living that feels like a natural part of each home.

Architecture

Architecture designed around terraces and views

Horizontal levels, planting and outdoor rooms help the buildings sit more naturally into the hillside.

The design focuses on shade, privacy, a calm arrival and good views without feeling overdone.

  • Layered terrace planning with generous exterior living space.
  • Mediterranean planting around the entrance and shared spaces.
  • Large windows positioned for light and views.
  • Boutique scale with a private residential feel.
